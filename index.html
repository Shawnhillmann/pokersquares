How to play
Swap any two cards to make poker hands. Hands can be either vertical or horizontal. Each attempt costs 100 credits.
How it works
Swap any two cards. After each swap, every row and column is evaluated as a 5-card poker hand. Any hand that’s at least TWO PAIR clears, cards fall, and new cards deal from the top—cascades can chain.
Straights support A-2-3-4-5 and 10-J-Q-K-A. Duplicate ranks can’t form a straight.
