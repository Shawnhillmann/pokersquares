How to play
Swap any two cards to make poker hands. Hands can be either vertical or horizontal. Each attempt costs 100 credits; hints cost 300 credits. Costs and hints scale with your current goal.
Run rewards
How it works
Swap any two cards. After each swap, every row and column is evaluated as a 5-card poker hand. Any hand that’s at least TWO PAIR clears, cards fall, and new cards deal from the top; cascades can chain.
Straights support A-2-3-4-5 and 10-J-Q-K-A. Duplicate ranks can’t form a straight.
Some goal rewards let you hold two pair or three of a kind so those lines do not clear. This enables higher scoring hands like full house or four of a kind.
